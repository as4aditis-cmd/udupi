Our Heritage

Where Every
Dish Tells a
Story

Nestled in the heart of ITO, Udupi Café brings the warmth and tradition of South Indian vegetarian cooking to Delhi's table. Every dish is prepared with the precision of generations and the love of home.

From our crisp Dosas to the soul-warming Sambar, from the tangy Bissi Bale Bath to the cooling Curd Rice — we honour the classics while crafting experiences that linger long after the last bite.
